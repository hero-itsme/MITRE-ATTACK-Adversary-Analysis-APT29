Threat Actor Profile — APT29

Overview
APT29, also known as Cozy Bear, is an advanced persistent threat group believed to be associated with Russia’s Foreign Intelligence Service (SVR). The group is primarily focused on cyber espionage campaigns.

Origin
- Russia (Suspected)

Targeted Sectors
- Government organizations
- Diplomatic entities
- Research institutes and think tanks
- Healthcare and vaccine research organizations
- Technology companies

Notable Campaigns
- SolarWinds supply chain compromise

Attack Characteristics
APT29 is known for stealthy, long-term intrusion campaigns involving spear-phishing, credential theft, supply chain attacks, and data exfiltration while maintaining low detection footprints.
