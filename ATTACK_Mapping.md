MITRE ATT&CK Tactic Mapping

Technique-to-Tactic Mapping

T1110 — Brute Force
- **Tactic:** Credential Access
- **Reason:** Used to obtain valid account credentials for further access.

T1001 — Data Obfuscation
- **Tactic:** Command and Control
- **Reason:** Used to hide communication between compromised hosts and attacker infrastructure.

T1036 — Masquerading
- **Tactic:** Defense Evasion
- **Reason:** Helps attackers avoid detection by appearing legitimate.
